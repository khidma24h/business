Microsoft OVS-ES Agreement for schools

The Microsoft Open Value Subscription Agreement for Education Solutions (OVS-ES Agreement) is designed for educational establishments with a Full-Time Equivalent (FTE) employees count of less than 1,000.

Please note: if you are a school with more than 100 FTEs, please speak to us before you sign up for an OVS-ES agreement. New licensing options for schools, including access to Enrolment for Education Solutions (EES) have become available in the last few months and are being piloted through a selected number of Microsoft partners, including Khidma24h. This enables schools to take advantage of competitive discounts on Microsoft 365 Education A3 and A5. Complete the contact form below for more information.

The Microsoft OVS-ES Agreement (or Open Value Subscription Agreement for Education Solutions) provides schools with:

  • An affordable volume-based price (count staff rather than devices) and rights to use the latest enterprise software products including Microsoft Office 2019/21, Windows 10/ 11, Microsoft Core/ Enterprise CAL Suites (Windows, Exchange, SharePoint CALs etc.), as well as free online services such as Office 365 Education and Microsoft 365 Apps for Enterprise (previously, Office 365 Professional Plus)
  • The simplicity of counting FTE employees just once per year
  • Software assurance: a powerful combination of benefits that include new version rights, the Home Use Program (HUP) and many other advantages including training resources for staff and students
  • A broad selection of additional Microsoft software products that may be licensed in any quantity, including servers
  • Free of charge access to the Imagine Academy programme, providing schools with tools and resources to enhance ‘Coding in the Curriculum’. Several certification exam vouchers per Microsoft EES, school, and OVS-ES volume licence agreement are replenished each year in November and each voucher can be redeemed via Certiport Testing to take one Microsoft certification exam: Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS), Microsoft Technology Associate (MTA), Microsoft Fundamentals, Microsoft Certified Educator (MCE). This helps educators prepare to pass Microsoft certification exams and earn Microsoft certification credentials at no additional cost
  • Predictable annual payments with a one-year or three-year term option
  • Simplified software licence management/ compliance

What are the minimum requirements to participate?

  • Customers with as few as five FTE employees can participate
  • Please speak to us first if you have more than 100 FTEs as you may be eligible to participate in EES
